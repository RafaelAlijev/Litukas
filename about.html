About Us

We are local photographers. We know how hard it is trying to make it here, and how competitive the industry can be. Our goal at Flash Studio is to provide affordable, friendly, and professional studio space rental for small to medium scale photo and video shoots. We use the space ourselves, and we are more than happy to share it.

Most of the clients our studios are regulars. They like having a comfortable and clean environment for their shoots and unlike the industry heavyweights, they can’t afford to shell out a million clams to do it.

Access

All spaces are two flights up, with wide staircases. We are happy to help you carry equipment up the stairs, and access to the building’s freight elevator can be arranged by appointment only Monday through Friday 7:30am to 3:30pm.

Street parking in our neighborhood has never been a problem. If you are traveling by train we are about 15 minutes from Leyton Station. For directions and maps, please click here.
